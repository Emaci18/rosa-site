About Me

I am a versatile professional with expertise in program leadership, curriculum development, higher education administration, teaching, and interdisciplinary research. My background includes both qualitative and quantitative research, with a focus on community-based participatory action research in the education and health sectors. I have a proven track record in program development, with numerous publications and international presentations. As a program administrator and principal investigator, I have successfully led educational and community-based programs, collaborating with educational leaders, community stakeholders, and policymakers to address the needs of underserved populations. My goal is to leverage my skills, research insights, and dedication to advancing the mission of a forward-looking organization.
